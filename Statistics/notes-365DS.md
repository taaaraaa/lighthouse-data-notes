# Skewness / Asymmetry
It indicates wether the data is concentrated on one side!
## 1. Right Skew 
-  It is tailing to the right side. 
- Mean> Median
- Outliers are on the right side

## 2. Zero Skew / No skew 
- Mean=median=mode
- Distribution is symmetrical
## 3. Left Skew/ Negative Skew
- Mean < Median
- Outliers on the left
